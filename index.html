Architecture UI Fair (AFAIR) is an annual event in a form of open house which is hosted by Architecture Student Council and Department of Architecture, Faculty of Engineering, University of Indonesia. For the 5th time, in 2014, AFAIR consistently present with a contextual theme for architectural world and another creative fields. AFAIR 2014 is wrapped in a series of events which are competition, lecture and discussion, social workshop, talkshow, and exhibition.
'Architecture for EveryONE'is a theme chosen in AFAIR 2014. By this theme, AFAIR tries to show the other side of design and architecture, about how design has to fulfill various and specific needs and desires of each individuals. The concept of space as body extension shows how a design is made with understandsing every special characteristics of each individuals.
On the other hand, in practice, architecture and design are often unable to fully respond the needs of each individual's variety special characteristics because of its costs and its productivity efficiency consideration.
With the theme "Architecture fo EveryONE", AFAIR 2014 tries to looking back the possibility of architecture starts from the smallest scale. Every design element was designed based on each individual. Each design has its "own selfhood". Every standards and methods that already exist can be turned into many possibilities.
it's not a matter of the sake of many people or individuals, but rather how the design can pass through the process of establishment based on its user's needs and desires.
